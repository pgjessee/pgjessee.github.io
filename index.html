Burn Bro Barbecue

Burn Bro Barbecue is an online restaurant meal ordering application. Customers can order food and leave a review about their experience. The target state for Burn Bro Barbecue to become a full-scale restaurant management application is currently in development. Employees of the restaurant will be able to better operate the restaurant through Burn Bro's data analytics, operations visibility, and HR capabilities.

Scarebnb

Scarebnb is an Airbnb inspired online vacation rental marketplace with a spooky twist. Users can book book real-life locations that are allegedly haunted. Users can proceed to leave a review about their stay and even their own location to rent.
